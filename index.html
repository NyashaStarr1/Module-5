The Noir Cases

A global true crime podcast and digital platform amplifying marginalized voices. Hosted by Nya Starr, each episode dives into cases affecting BIPOC communities. Blending research, cultural context and and storytelling while bridging the gap between the criminal justice system and the communities it serves.

📌 Featured Case

Jaliek’s Note

Case Type: Missing ● Status: Ongoing ● Location: New York.

When 12 year old Jaliek Rainwalker was last seen on November 2, 2007. Labeled "troubled" and shuffled between adoptive homes, Jaliek left behind a cryptic note that raised more questions than answers.This episode explores the unanswered questions, shifting stories, and unusual behaviors that followed from his adoptive family.

📰 Recent Updates

S1 • E8

Case Update: New developments in the Asha Degree case

On February 14, 2000, 9-year-old Asha Degree disappeared from her North Carolina home. Twenty-five years later, new developments may finally bring answers in one of the state’s most haunting mysteries.

S2 • E6

Cold Case: What Happened to Marsha “Pay It No Mind” Johnson

Black trans activist Marsha “Pay It No Mind” Johnson was found in the Hudson River in 1992, ruled a suicide by police. We revisit her life, legacy, and the unanswered questions surrounding her death.

S1 • E7

Survivor Stories: Warning Shot — The Marissa Alexander Story

Marissa Alexander faced a 20-year sentence after firing a warning shot to protect herself from her abusive husband. We examine her case, Florida’s “Stand Your Ground” law, and the justice system’s racial and gender disparities.
